About Us


What is ICanCook?
Our web application creates a platform for the community to post and share healthy cooking recipes with a free and easy to access vast recipe list. Our main focus is to carry out a reverse search and start cooking with the ingredients u have rather than making frequent stops to the store. This does not only save time, but also money. We all are students and know how difficult it can be to start cooking. Thus, we will experiment this site and make it open to students - to reduce the consumption of unhealthy food , before launching it to the general public

Who are we?
We are a group of students in CMPT 470 Group-13 Fall 2016.
