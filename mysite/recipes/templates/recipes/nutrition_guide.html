Nutrition Guide

Our Nutrition Guide will tell you about the nutrition of a particular product. This information includes serving size, calories per serving and the amount of various nutrients contained in the product. Once you have an understanding about these products you can follow a healthy diet and it is comparatively easier to choose the most nutritious foods.

Importance of Nutrition Guide

Nutrition facts is needed to make healthy food choices.

Standardized serving sizes to be able to make nutritional comparisons.

Information about the amount per serving of nutrients such as saturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, sugar, and fiber; these are very important for people with different health concerns.

Nutrient reference values or the percent daily values help you see how a particular food item fits into your daily meal plan. It also shows how much of these nutrients you are getting to meet your daily requirements.
